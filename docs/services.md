# Services & Monetization

StatForge is built to provide rigorous, automated statistical reporting to both independent researchers and scale-out institutional labs. 

We offer two distinct deployment and analysis levels.

## 1. Open Source (Free)
The core CLI provides free, unrestricted access to the automated statistical pipeline.
*   **Included:** Full CLI `statforge run` pipeline execution, model registry access, and standardized report generation.

## 2. Custom Solutions (Custom Quote)
We provide professional integration and development services for labs and institutions.
*   **Bespoke Models:** Development of custom PyMC or Frequentist plugins tailored to specific research questions.
*   **Institutional Deployment:** Server-side installation, SSO integration, and branded templates.
*   **Training & Support:** Dedicated workshops and priority engineering support for high-stakes analysis.
